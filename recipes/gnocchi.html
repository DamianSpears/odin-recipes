Gnocchi Recipe

Description

Gnocchi is a potato based pasta that is considered delicious by many. Not only is it delicious, but it is also extremely versatile. This type of pasta can be dressed in many different sauces and paired with a variety of proteins. Some Recipes have even called for it to be cooked twice before serving.

While there are many options for preparing gnocchi, we are going to focus on a popular approach: pesto gnocchi. Pesto is a sauce derived from oils and herbs and has an fresh, aromatic scent to it. Pesto is a mild, rich sauce that has little to no spice. Pesto can be paired with a variety of meats including shrimp, sausage, chicken, and lamb. In this recipe, we will be using shrimp as our protein.

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi
  • shrimp
  • pesto
  • olive oil
  • Light Cream
  1. Boil the gnocchi
  2. While the gnocchi is coming to a boil, begin preparing shrimp
  3. Once gnocchi begins to float, it is time to drain
  4. In a saucepan, add pesto, olive oil, and light cream
  5. Combine all ingredients and add salt and pepper to taste
